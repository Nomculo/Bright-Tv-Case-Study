*BRIGHT TV CASE STUDY ANALYSIS.*

This case study aims to identify BrightTV's subscriber and viewership data to verify usage trends, understand the factors influencing consumption, and provide recommendations to increase subscriptions and viewer engagement.

*PROJECT PROJECTIVES*
1. What content is most popular at Bright Tv?
2. Which day has the lowest and highest watching hours throughtout the week?
3. What factors influence consumption?
4. What type of content should Bright Tv be recommending?
5. What ways can Bright Tv use to attract more subscribers?

*TOOLS USED*
1. Databricks
2. SQL
3. Chatgpt
4. Canva
5. Miro

*PROCESS*

1. Used Miro to map out the whole process of analyzing the data given. The mapping process includes asking data driven questions to come with solutions that best help Bright TV.
